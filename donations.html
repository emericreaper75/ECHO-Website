Where Your Donation Goes

40% of donations fund new oscilloscopes, 3D printers, soldering stations, and other essential tools.

25% supports scholarships for students who can't afford membership fees or conference attendance.

20% covers registration fees, travel, and materials for hackathons and robotics competitions.

15% funds workshops, guest speakers, and STEM outreach programs for local schools.
