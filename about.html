About ECHO Club

Electronics & Communication Heuristic Organisation — Built by students, for students, since 2016

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization that bridges the gap between academic excellence and real-world innovation in electronics and computing. We envision a community where every student feels empowered to explore, experiment, and excel in the ever-evolving world of technology.

🎯

Our Values

Innovation, collaboration, inclusivity, hands-on learning, and continuous improvement guide everything we do. We believe that the best learning happens when students work together on real problems, supported by mentors who care about their growth.

💡

Our Impact

Creating positive change through meaningful projects, STEM outreach, and preparing students for successful careers in technology. From national hackathon victories to community-serving IoT solutions, our members are making a difference that extends beyond the campus.

🌍

What We Do

⚡ Electronics Projects

Design and build circuits, PCBs, embedded systems, and IoT devices using professional-grade equipment in our dedicated lab.

💻 Software Development

Learn programming languages, web development, mobile apps, AI/ML, and cloud technologies through structured workshops.

🤖 Robotics

Build autonomous robots, drones, and mechatronic systems. Compete in regional and national robotics competitions.

🎓 Workshops & Training

Weekly workshops covering technical skills, professional development, and industry best practices with expert mentors.

🏆 Hackathons & Competitions

Host and participate in hackathons, CTF competitions, and engineering challenges at state and national levels.

🤝 Industry Connections

Networking events, guest speakers, company tours, and internship opportunities with top tech companies.

Our Facilities

🔧

Electronics Lab

Fully equipped with oscilloscopes, signal generators, power supplies, soldering stations, and component inventory.

🖨️

Fabrication Center

3D printers, laser cutter, CNC machine, and PCB milling equipment for rapid prototyping.

🖥️

Computer Workstations

High-performance workstations with development tools, simulation software, and design applications.

📚

Resource Library

Technical books, online course subscriptions, and documentation for continuous learning.
