ECHO Overview

The Electronics and Communication Heuristic Organisation (ECHO) was established to foster a vibrant community of students passionate about electronics, computing, and emerging technologies at JNTUACEA. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and collaborative innovation, we bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world application.

The club offers workshops, hackathons, guest lectures, and project mentorship across a wide range of technical domains. With access to a fully equipped electronics lab, fabrication center, and computer workstations, members gain practical skills that prepare them for successful careers in technology.
