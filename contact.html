Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join ECHO Club?

Attend one of our weekly meetings on Wednesdays at 6 PM. Fill out a membership form and pay the $20 semester fee. That's it!

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all! We welcome beginners and experts alike. Our workshops start from the basics and we have mentors to help you learn.

What's the time commitment?

As much or as little as you want! Most members attend 1-2 events per week, but active project participants may spend more time in the lab.

Can non-students join?

Yes! We offer community memberships for $50/semester. Alumni can also join our alumni network. Contact us for details.

Ready to Get Started?

Visit us during our weekly meeting or drop by during lab hours
